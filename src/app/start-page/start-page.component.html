Kuber Owner
Kuber Charities Inc. is a US corporation which owns the technology platform to digitally transform the process of gifting to charities worldwide.
Kuber Registered Office
Kuber Charities is headquartered in the US with its registered office in Pennsylvania.
Motto and Purpose
Kuber’s mission is to leverage technology to its highest potential to enable its users to make donation gifts to charities of their choice with complete ease and track their gifts all year round.
